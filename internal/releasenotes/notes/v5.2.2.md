Clearer setup for Unraid notifications.

## Fixed

- **Unraid notifications no longer look like they depend on the VM libvirt test.** They run over the SSH connection you set up under *VM Backup over SSH*, but they do **not** need libvirt or VMs. The hint now says so, and the *Test connection* there — when the SSH link works but libvirt is absent — now spells out that the SSH connection itself is fine and libvirt is only for VM backups. To check notifications, use **Send test** on the Notifications tab. (#53)
