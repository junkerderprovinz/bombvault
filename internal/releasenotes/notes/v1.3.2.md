Small UX polish for the domain tabs.

## Changed
- **Disabled domain tabs are now hidden, not greyed out.** The VMs and Flash tabs only appear in the sidebar once you enable their domain (Settings → Domains) — a cleaner sidebar that shows only what's actually in use.
- **Enabling a domain updates the sidebar instantly — no page reload.** Toggling VMs or Flash on/off and saving now reveals or hides the tab immediately.
