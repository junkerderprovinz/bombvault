## Changed

- **Local snapshot retention moved to Paths & Storage.** In the v5 layout the local "keep last / daily / weekly / monthly" controls were grouped with the off-site retention under the **Off-site** tab, which made them hard to find. They now sit in their own **Snapshot retention** card on the **Paths & Storage** tab, next to the backup paths they prune. The Off-site tab keeps a dedicated **Off-site retention** card for the off-site repo. (#51)
